How to Install
  1. Download the APK.
  2. Open the downloaded file from your browser or Downloads app.
  3. If blocked: Settings → Security/Privacy → Install unknown apps → allow your browser/file manager.
  4. Tap Install.
  5. Open the app from your launcher.
Tip: Open the app once with internet ON, then it will work offline after caching.
